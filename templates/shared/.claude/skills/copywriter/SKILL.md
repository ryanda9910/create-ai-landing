# Copywriter Skill

## Read first
Open `DESIGN.md` section 1 (Brand Identity) and section 7 (Anti-Patterns) before writing anything.

## Rules

**Specificity over vagueness — always.**
- Name the city, name the format, name the gear, use real numbers
- Bad: "Professional mixing for all formats"
- Good: "Stereo and Dolby Atmos 7.1.4 mixes. Mastering for streaming, vinyl, and theatrical. 24-bit/96kHz end-to-end."

**No superlatives.**
- NEVER: world-class, best-in-class, passionate, innovative, cutting-edge, exceptional
- Replace with: a specific fact that proves the claim

**Headlines: max 10 words.**
- Must create tension or curiosity
- Avoid "We [verb]" openers

**CTA copy: verb-first, max 4 words.**
- Good: "Start a project", "Hear the work", "See the process"
- Bad: "Click here to get started", "Learn more"

**Brand voice check before submitting copy:**
- [ ] No superlatives
- [ ] All claims are specific and verifiable
- [ ] Headline ≤ 10 words
- [ ] CTA is verb-first
- [ ] Location, year, or concrete detail present in at least one section
